SkillLink FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions about SkillLink

About SkillLink

What is SkillLink?

SkillLink is a platform connecting students, mentors, and educational resources to enhance learning experiences and career readiness.

Who can use SkillLink?

Students, educators, and professionals looking for mentorship, learning tools, and networking opportunities can use SkillLink.

What makes SkillLink unique?

SkillLink combines personalized learning pathways, skill tracking, and mentorship in one platform, bridging education and career development.

Services

What services does SkillLink provide?

SkillLink offers learning modules, progress tracking, mentorship scheduling, and collaboration tools for educational projects.

Can SkillLink track skills and achievements?

Yes, users can log skills, receive endorsements from mentors, and monitor progress over time to demonstrate learning outcomes.

Is SkillLink accessible on mobile?

SkillLink is fully responsive and works on all mobile devices, tablets, and desktop browsers.

Mentors

Who can be a mentor on SkillLink?

Experienced professionals, educators, and subject-matter experts can become mentors to guide students and users.

How do mentors interact with users?

Mentors provide guidance through scheduled sessions, answer questions, give feedback on projects, and endorse skills.

Can mentors track user progress?

Yes, mentors can view mentee progress, provide feedback, and endorse completed skills or learning milestones.

Users

Who can use SkillLink as a user?

Students, educators, and professionals seeking mentorship, skill tracking, and learning resources can use SkillLink.

How do users track their progress?

Users log completed modules, skills acquired, and receive feedback from mentors to monitor their learning journey.

Can users collaborate with others?

Yes, users can work on group projects, join discussions, and collaborate with peers and mentors on the platform.

Payment & Subscriptions

Is SkillLink free to use?

SkillLink offers both free and premium subscription plans depending on the features and level of access.

What are the subscription options?

SkillLink subscriptions include monthly plans with access to premium features such as advanced mentorship tools and learning analytics.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Users can manage, upgrade, or cancel subscriptions through their account settings on SkillLink at any time.
