What is SkilkLink?

SkillLink is a mentorship and skill-sharing platform connecting students, professionals, and lifelong learners. Think of us as a mix between LinkedIn and Calendly!

Our goal is to make learning and personal growth simple, interactive, and rewarding.

Earn Badges

Receive badges or certifications for completed sessions to showcase your achievements.
